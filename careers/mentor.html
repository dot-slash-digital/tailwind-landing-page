Software Engineering

We founded Tailwind Talent to redefine the job search process, so students can have an enjoyable experience in starting their career, not a stressful one. As a mentor, you will be their guide in navigating the convoluted mess that is the process of finding a job/internship. Together, we have the opportunity to change lives for the better and to open their eyes to the beautiful world of software engineering.

What you'll be doing
  • Provide advice on the job search process, interviewing, negotiating, comparing offers, how to make the most of their internship/new job, and more
  • Learn about your client's interests and career goals to help the Tailwind Team find companies and position types that would be a good fit for them
  • Critique your client's resume and cover letter, and provide suggestions for improvement
  • Prep your client (as needed) for behavioral, technical, and system design interviews
  • Guide your client through the process of negotiating offers and help them create a negotiation strategy
  • Find housing resources to aid your client's search in housing for their upcoming internship/full-time job
  • Time commitment: Approximately 8 hours per month for 4 months per client, depending on your client(s)' needs
  • Start date: First week of August 2021
Compensation

At Tailwind, we're committed to fair and transparent compensation. We want our clients to start their career on the right path, and that can't happen without you. When determining our compensation tiers, we aimed to balance the value that you provide with the amount that our clients are paying for our services.

To make it fair for all mentors and to reduce any potential bias, we have two compensation tiers:

  • College mentors
    • You're currently a university student or you've already graduated but have not started working full-time
    • You will be mentoring students seeking internships and/or co-ops
    • $15/hour per client, plus a 66% bonus for each client you help land an internship/co-op
  • Industry mentors
    • You're currently working full-time as a software engineer (or similar role)
    • You will be mentoring students graduating in Fall 2021 or Spring 2022 seeking new grad opportunities
    • $20/hour per client, plus a 100% bonus for each client you help land a full-time job
What we're looking for
  • You're currently a university student or working as a full-time software engineer (or similar role)
  • If you're a university student, you have at least 1 internship under your belt
  • You're passionate about mentoring future software engineers and sharing your experiences
  • You want future engineers to have a better job search experience than you had
  • (optional) You have prior mentoring experience, in a collegiate or industry setting
  • U.S. citizenship is required
About us

We didn't start Tailwind because we enjoy recruiting, but rather because of how shitty it is. It's the best feeling in the world to get your first job offer straight out of college, but the process of getting to that point is downright miserable. We, personally, were lucky enough to have met the right people in our lives that put us on the right path for starting our careers, but not everyone gets that opportunity. Not only do we want to pass that experience on for our clients, but we want to make it better. The future generation of software engineers deserve better, and we're here to make that a reality.
